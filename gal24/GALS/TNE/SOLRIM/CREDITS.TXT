Solomani Rim - Credits
----------------------

The Classic Era Solomani Rim was published in Supplement 10,
The Solomani Rim, put out by Game Designers' Workshop in 1982.
This book lists the following credits:

  Design . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . John Harshman
  Assistance & Good Ideas. . . Loren Wiseman & John Astell
  Art Director . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  Paul R. Banner
  Asst. Art Director . . . . . . . . . . . . Chris Purcell

Harold Hale has updated the sector for the New Era, and this
data has been put into Galactic-format by Lewis Roberts.
