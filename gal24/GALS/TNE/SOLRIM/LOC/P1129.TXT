Ishkur B5629C9-9            
Starport: Good              Size: Medium (7200-8800 km)
Atmosphere: Standard        Hydrographics: 20% water
Population: 1 billion       Govt: Charismatic Oligarchy
Law Level: High             Technology: Early Stellar

Ishkur has been courted by both the Dingir League and the Terran Republic to
become a member, but has turned both down. Ishkur prefers to remain a neutral
meeting ground and trading center to both sides, and since the incident at
Meshan, has served in this capacity on several occasions.
