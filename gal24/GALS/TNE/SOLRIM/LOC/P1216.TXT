    During the Collapse human supremacists first restricted the Vegan 
population to special districts, then slaughtered them in the wake of the 
Collapse. Inhabitants of Ludmilla are very reluctant to discuss the chain of
events that lead to the massacres, but evidence indicates that perhaps the 
human communities suffered disproportionate losses in the wake of the Virus 
attacks, and some how blamed the Vegans for their misfortune.
