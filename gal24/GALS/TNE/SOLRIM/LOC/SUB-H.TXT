
Banasdan Subsector
------------------

Atria (Banasdan) Subsector: The Atria Subsector has an estimated
population in 1202 of 17.66 billion, a decline of 92 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 7.53 billion at
Ludovic, and its highest tech level is 9 at Sathyos and Lakamsal.

Atria (Banasdan) Subsector (historical): The Banasdan Subsector had an
estimated population in 1117 of approximately 219.1 billion. Its highest
population was 74.8 billion at Ganesh, and its highest tech level was 15
at Catania, Noricum, Banasdan, and Cephesa.
