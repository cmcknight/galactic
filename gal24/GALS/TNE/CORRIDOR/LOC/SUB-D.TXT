
THE NARROWS

The Narrows Subsector: The Narrows Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 6.75 billion, a decline of 59 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 5.69 billion at Uerrgno,
and its highest tech level is 7 at Ackaeck and Atudew.

The Narrows Subsector (historical): The Narrows Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 16.3 billion. Its highest population
was 8.45 billion at Plunge, and its highest tech level was 15 at Dilub Rou.
