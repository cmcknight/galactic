
SHUSH

Shush Subsector: Shush has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately
31,700, a decline of 51 percent from its Pre-Collapse population.

Shush Subsector (historical): Shush had an estimated population in 1117 of
approximately 65,200.

Subsector Notes

The few remaining inhabitants of this Rift world would eventually die out
with their depleted gene pool. The starport has been left untouched for 60
years and has only suffered from lack of maintenance.
