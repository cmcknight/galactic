
STRAND

Strand Subsector: Strand Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of
approximately 780.65 million, a decline of 96 percent from its Pre-Collapse
population. Its highest population is 768 million at Zudagim, and its
highest tech level is 9 at Zinadura and Ashima.

Strand Subsector (historical): Strand Subsector had an estimated population
in 1117 of approximately 18.62 billion. Its highest population was 8.57
billion at Ashima, and its highest tech level was 15 at Toddie-lee and
Depot.
