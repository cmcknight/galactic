
SINTA

Sinta Subsector: Sinta Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of
approximately 1.41 billion, a decline of 75 percent from its Pre-Collapse
population. Its highest population is 717 million at Inarli, and its
highest tech level is 9 also at Inarli, Kateri-Divad and Tahspeck.

Sinta Subsector (historical): Sinta Subsector had an estimated population
in 1117 of approximately 5.59 billion. Its highest population was 4.48
billion at Inarli, and its highest tech level was 15 at Kysar II.
