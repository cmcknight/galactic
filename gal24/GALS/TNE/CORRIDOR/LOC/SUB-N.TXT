
ASHISHINIPAR

Ashishinipar Subsector: Ashishinipar Subsector has an estimated population
in 1202 of approximately 4.71 billion, a decline of 7 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 4.63 billion at
Ashishinipar, and its highest tech level is 8 also at Ashishinipar.

Ashishinipar Subsector (historical): Ashishinipar Subsector had an
estimated population in 1117 of approximately 5.05 billion. Its highest
population was 4.97 billion at Ashishinipar, and its highest tech level was
9 also at Ashishinipar.
