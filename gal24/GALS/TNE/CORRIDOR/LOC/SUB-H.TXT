
UANTIL

Uantil Subsector: The worlds of Uantil Subsector are completely devoid of
sentient life in 1202.

Uantil Subsector (historical): Uantil Subsector had an estimated population
in 1117 of approximately 861.7 million. Its highest population was 853
million at Naagasa, and its highest tech level was 9 at Naagasa and Mee.

Subsector Notes

The uncompromising atmospheres of Uantil's systems has made its worlds
completely uninhabitable by humans.
