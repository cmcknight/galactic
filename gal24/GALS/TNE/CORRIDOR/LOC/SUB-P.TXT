
Sashrakusha

Sashrakusha Subsector: Sashrakusha Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 2.45 billion, a decline of 76 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 823 million at
Nart-roft, and its highest tech level is 7 at Umaraag, Tant, Radical,
Sepstar, Yull-jettii and Idanchy.

Sashrakusha Subsector (historical): Sashrakusha Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 10.14 billion. Its highest population
was 7.37 billion at Kanorb, and its highest tech level was 15 at Sumeszu
and Palama.
