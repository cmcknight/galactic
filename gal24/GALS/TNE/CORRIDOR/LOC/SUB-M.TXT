
TWO WORLDS

Two Worlds Subsector: Two Worlds Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 7.32 million, a decline of 97 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 7.32 million at Esi-obe,
and its highest tech level is 9 also at Esi-obe.

Two Worlds Subsector (historical): Two Worlds Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 275 million. Its highest population was
275 million at Esi-obe, and its highest tech level was 12 also at Esi-obe.
