
KHUKISH

Khukish Subsector: The Khukish Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 2.45 billion, a decline of 96 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 972 million at
Zontra'Lee, and its highest tech level is 10 at Drayne.

Khukish Subsector (historical): The Khukish Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 65.39 billion. Its highest population
was 27.9 billion at Durima, and its highest tech level was 15 at Shushaka,
Durima and Khukish.
