
KIVU

Kivu Subsector: Kivu Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of
approximately 14.43 million, a decline of 84 percent from its Pre-Collapse
population. Its highest population is 5.28 million at Tucker, and its
highest tech level is 6 at Emm.

Kivu Subsector (historical): Kivu Subsector had an estimated population in
1117 of approximately 90.25 million. Its highest population was 72.4
million at Uiolksdah, and its highest tech level was 12 at Uiolksdah.

Subsector Notes

This small interstellar community has made occasional with spinward Vilani
states and independent worlds. Answerin scouts have made minimal contact
with the Center and Emm systems.
