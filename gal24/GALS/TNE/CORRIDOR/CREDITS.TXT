Corridor Sector - Credits
-------------------------

The Original version of the Corridor sector was published in
issue 18 of The Travellers' Digest put out by Digest Group
Publications in January of 1990. The people involved in the
Corridor write-up from that issue were James Holden, Phillip
Athans, Mike Mikesh, and David Riddell.

This sector was later updated by GDW in the Regency Sourcebook.
Additional work is by Chris Griffen.
