
21 Worlds Subsector:
--------------------

21 Worlds subsector has a New Era population of just over 6.21 billion, a
decline of over 104.7 billion from its pre-Collapse population.  Its highest
population is 6 billion, at Bonsher, and its highest tech level is 9, at
Jrack.  Only the world of Bonsher has experienced a population increase over
its pre-Collapse level.
