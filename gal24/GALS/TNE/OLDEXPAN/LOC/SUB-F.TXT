
Jayna Subsector:
----------------

Jayna subsector has a New Era population of just over 13.2 billion, a decline
of over 18.6 billion from its pre-Collapse population. Its highest
population is 10 billion, at Czerniak, and its highest tech level is 9, at
Galines.  Five worlds have actually experienced a population increase over
their pre-Collapse levels: Aneth, Czerniak, Kata, Thanatos, and Galines.
