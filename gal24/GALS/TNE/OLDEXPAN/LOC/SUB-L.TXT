
Vendtup Subsector
-----------------

Vendtup subsector has a New Era population of just over 120 million, a
decline of over 10.7 billion from its pre-Collapse population. Its highest
population is 80 million, at Urbaine, and its highest tech level is 8, at
Vreeland and New Bond. Only Vreeland has experienced a population increase
over its pre-Collapse level.
