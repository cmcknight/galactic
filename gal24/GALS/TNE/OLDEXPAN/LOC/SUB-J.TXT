
Shenk Subsector
---------------

Shenk subsector has a New Era population of just over 12.9 billion, a decline
of over 46 billion from its pre-Collapse population. Its highest population
is 8 billion, at Yontez, and its highest tech level is 9, at Mueller and
Solee. Three worlds have actually experienced a population increase over
their pre-collapse levels: Janie, Mueller, and Clallum.
