
Aubaine Subsector
-----------------

Aubaine subsector (renamed from Nicosia) has a population in 1201 of just
over 1.38 billion, a decline from pre-Final War levels of almost 5.4
billion. Its highest population is 800 million, at Aubaine, and its highest
tech level is 12(C) also at Aubaine. Aubaine subsector is home to the
Reformation Coalition, and earlier, the Dawn League, whose capital is at
Aubaine. The Coalition has repopulated three world in Aubaine subsector:
Rohit, Apollo, and Kruyter. Since their original populations had all died,
they are classified as cemetery worlds.
