
Introduction to the New Era Galaxy
----------------------------------

This is the beginning of a "New Era" galaxy which Lewis Roberts
(lewis@chara.gsu.edu) is busy putting together with the help of
Eamon Watters (E.Watters@queens-belfast.ac.uk). Thanks to Leroy
Guatney (lwlguatn@ouray.cudenver.edu) for the Spica data. The
Spinward Marches along with various BARD write-ups is also
included. If you'd like to contribute, contact Lewis at the
address given above.
