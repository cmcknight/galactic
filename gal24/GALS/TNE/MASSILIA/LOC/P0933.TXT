Jacat C3524P2-9

Starport: Routine               Size: Small (4000-5600 km)
Atmosphere: Thin                Hydrographics: 20% water
Population: 50,000              Govt: Charismatic Dictators
Law Level: Low                  Technology: Early Stellar
      
This world is the home base for the Blue Pearl corsair band, which preys
mainly on starships from the nearby Geonee Confederation and from Nagiida.
The flagship of the fleet is the Triple Fear, a still operative patrol
cruiser from Rebellion times.
