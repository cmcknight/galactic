Udipeni X6879WC-7

Starport: None             Size: Medium (8800-10400 km)
Atmosphere: Dense          Hydrographics: 70% water
Population: 2 billion      Govt: Self Perpetuating Oligarch
Law Level: Extreme         Technology: Pre-Stellar
      	
         Udipeni, famous in pre-Rebellion times for its Iilodav Music
Festival, has experienced an 80% population decrease with respect to that
times. The charismatic oligarchy which originally governed the world has
evolved into four separate, fighting groups which hold power in their
respective regions through control of relic technology.
