Deneb Sector - Credits
----------------------

The original version of the Deneb sector was published in
in issue 19 of The Travellers' Digest put out by Digest
Group Publications in May of 1990. The people involved in
the Deneb write-up from that issue were James Holden, Mike
Mikesh, and Nancy Parker.

This sector was later updated by GDW in the Regency Sourcebook.
Additional work is by Chris Griffen.
