
Fervore Subsector
-----------------

Fervore Subsector: Fervore Subsector has an estimated population in 1202
of approximately 625 million, a decline of over 99 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 434 million, at Cirnam,
and its highest tech level is 8 at Uvareck and Cirnam.

Fervore Subsector (historical): Fervore Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 127.09 billion. Its highest population
was 81.65 billion at Duncan, and its highest tech level was 14 at Cyan and
11 other worlds.
