
Addison Subsector
-----------------

Addison Subsector: The Addison Subsector has an estimated population
in 1202 of approximately 2.14 billion, a decline of 48 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 808 million at
Tihiin, and its highest tech level is 7 at Yuno and Vilon.

Addison Subsector (historical): The Addison Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 4.12 billion. Its highest population
was 2.03 billion at Addison, and its highest tech level was 14 at Addison
and nine other worlds.
