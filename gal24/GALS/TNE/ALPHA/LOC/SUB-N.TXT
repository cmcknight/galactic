
Regulus Subsector
-----------------

Regulus Subsector: The Regulus Subsector has an estimated population
in 1202 of approximately 306 million, a decline of 92 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 286 million at
Pijo, and its highest tech level is 8 at Dimpo Iu.

Regulus Subsector (historical): The Regulus Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 3.61 billion. Its highest population
was 3.33 billion at Tino, and its highest tech level was 14 at Tino and
eight other worlds.
