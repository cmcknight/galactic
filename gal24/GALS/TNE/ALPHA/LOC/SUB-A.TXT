
Lazzerton Subsector
-------------------

1200: Lazzerton Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of
approximately 711.5 million, a decline of 99 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 640 million at
Umkarguu, and its highest tech level is 9 at Keats. 

The Lazzerton (Ximenes) Subsector (1120): Ximenes Subsector had an
estimated population in 1117 of approximately 68.95 billion. Its highest
population was 31.2 billion at Faro, and its highest tech level was 15 at
Carbuncle, Iamir, Wulfe, Faro, Marathon and Hale.
