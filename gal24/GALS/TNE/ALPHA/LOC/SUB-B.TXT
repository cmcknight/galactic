
McKenzie Subsector
------------------

McKenzie Subsector: McKenzie Subsector has an estimated population in 1202
of approximately 122 million, a decline of over 99 percent from its
Pre-Collapse  population. Its highest population is 80 million at Wabash,
and its highest tech level is 9 at Dixie.

McKenzie Subsector (historical): McKenzie Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 119.2 billion. Its highest population
was 80 billion at Fairday, and its highest tech level was 15 at Ychanon,
Patrent, Grady, Port Royal and Surrey.
