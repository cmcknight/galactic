
Vald Subsector
--------------

Vald Subsector: The Vald Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of
approximately 671 million, an increase of 4 percent from its Pre-Collapse
population. Its highest population is 643 million at Teta Lise, and its
highest tech level is 7 at Pesacce.

Vald Subsector (historical): The Vald Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 647 million. Its highest population
was 516 million at Teta Lise, and its highest tech level was 14 at Dalmina
and six other worlds.
