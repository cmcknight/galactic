
Orichalc Subsector
------------------

Orichalc Subsector: Orichalc Subsector has an estimated population in 1202
of approximately 1.749 billion, a decline of 98 percent from its
Pre-Collapse  population. Its highest population is 643 million at Summit,
and its highest tech level is 9 at Orichalc, Angurvadel and Phoummathep.

Orichalc Subsector (historical): Orichalc Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 82.41 billion. Its highest population
was 76.2 billion at Cthonia, and its highest tech level was 15 at Sharif,
Toonge and Quetzalcoatl.
