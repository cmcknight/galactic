
Oriah Subsector
----------------

Oriah Subsector: Oriah Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of
approximately 287 million, a decline of over 99 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 212 million, at Orbdir,
and it's highest tech level is 9 also at Orbdir.

Oriah Subsector (historical): Oriah Subsector had an estimated population
in 1117 of approximately 162 billion. Its highest population was 91.32
billion at Weeven, and its highest tech level was 14 at Weeven and six
other worlds.
