
Ziusudra Subsector
------------------

Ziusudra Subsector: The Ziusudra Subsector has an estimated population
in 1202 of approximately 2.08 billion, a decline of 97 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 1.14 billion at
Balboa, and its highest tech level is 7 at Yuno and Vilon.

Ziusudra Subsector (historical): The Ziusudra Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 79.3 billion. Its highest population
was 70.09 billion at Scathe, and its highest tech level was 14 at Scathe
and five other worlds.
