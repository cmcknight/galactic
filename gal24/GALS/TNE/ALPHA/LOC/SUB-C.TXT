
Dagir Subsector
---------------

Dagir Subsector:</B> Dagir Subsector has an estimated population in 1202
of approximately 2.88 billion, a decline of over 99 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 841 million at Clerk's
Realm, and its highest tech level is 7 at Zephir, Defiance, Dagir, Ke-aday
and Valhalla.
 
Dagir Subsector (historical):</B>Dagir Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 22.79 billion. Its highest population
was 8.5 billion at Killbrouha, and its highest tech level was 14 at Occam,
Heaven Belt, Killbrouha, Gemstar, Perstein, Peodein, Clavis Nine, Cloudia,
Azure and Harland.
