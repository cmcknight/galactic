
VOSKHOD

Voskhod Subsector: The Voskhod Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 552.88 million, a decline of 96 percent from its
Pre-Collapse popu-lation. Its highest population is 480 million at
Kegirrur, and its highest tech level is 9 at Maluuf.

Voskhod Subsector (historical): The Voskhod Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 12.33 billion. Its highest population
was 9.6 billion at Kegirrur, and its highest tech level was 15 at Shaddukan
and Voskhod.
