
THAGGESH

Thaggesh Subsector: The Thaggesh Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 1.06 billion, a decline of 93 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 535 million at Luukon,
and its highest tech level is 9 at Thaggesh.

Thaggesh (Anakod) Subsector (historical): The Thaggesh (Anakod) Subsector
had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 16.23 billion. Its
highest population was 7.9 billion at Jana, and its highest tech level was
15 at Arklin, Anakod and Diikaras.
