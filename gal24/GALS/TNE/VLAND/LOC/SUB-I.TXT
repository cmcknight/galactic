
DUSA

Dusa Subsector: The Dusa Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of
approximately 83 million, a decline of 84 percent from its Pre-Collapse
popu-lation. Its highest population is 26.1 million at Khulekii, and its
highest tech level is 10 at Pirrom.

Dusa Subsector (historical): The Dusa Subsector had an estimated population
in 1117 of approximately 525.6 million. Its highest population was 240
million at Keshiil, and its highest tech level was 15 at Niirka.
