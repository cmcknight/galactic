
APURSHISH

Apurshish (Akumid) Subsector: The Apurshish Subsector has an estimated
population in 1202 of approximately 1.15 billion, a decline of 90 percent
from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 736 million at
Apurshish, and its highest tech level is 9 at Apurshish.

Apurshish (Akumid) Subsector (historical): The Apurshish (Akumid) Subsector
had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 11.01 billion. Its
highest population was 6.85 billion at Apurshish, and its highest tech
level was 15 at Robbuun, Bakog and Akumid.
