
KALAALIT

Kalaalit Subsector: The Kalaalit Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 8.2 billion, a decline of 93 percent from its
Pre-Collapse popu-lation. Its highest population is 5.75 billion at
Inushir, and its highest tech level is 10 at Kalaalit.

Kalaalit (Kakadan) Subsector (historical): The Kalaalit (Kakadan) Subsector
had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 125.85 billion. Its
highest population was 79.2 billion at Kane, and its highest tech level was
15 at Kiddinu, Kesali, Sg'aa, Icuspin and Punnari.
