
ANARSI

Anarsi Subsector: The Anarsi Subsector has an estimated population in 1202
of approximately 372.2 million, a decline of 98 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 28.7 million at
Sadniben, and its highest tech level is 9 at Newcastle.

Anarsi Subsector (historical): The Anarsi Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 18.76 billion. Its highest population
was 9.15 billion at Anarsi, and its highest tech level was 16 at Gemid.
