
KAGAMIRA

Kagamira Subsector: The Kagamira Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 208.03 million, a decline of 96 percent from its
Pre-Collapse popu-lation. Its highest population is 76.3 million at Vallae,
and its highest tech level is 7 at Thogho, Astira, Ersii and Midku.

Kagamira Subsector (historical): The Kagamira Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 7.44 billion. Its highest population
was 5.65 billion at Kagamira, and its highest tech level was 15 at Gukhaga
and Kagamira.
