
THETON

Theton Subsector: The Theton Subsector has an estimated population in 1202
of approximately 912.4 million, a decline of 95 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 657 million at
Vutsarrgh, and its highest tech level is 6 at Daztsoun, Spinport, Zupilak
and Veppim.

Theton Subsector (historical): The Theton Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 20.07 billion. Its highest population
was 9.04 billion at Arga, and its highest tech level was 15 at Raltaedz.

Subsector Notes

On the spinward edge of Lishun Sector, Theton was hit relatively hard by
Virus and the Collapse. This subsector remains adrift in the wilds with
little or no contact with any worlds outside its boundaries.
