
ISLE

Isle Subsector: The Isle Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of
approximately 1.6 billion, a decline of 99 percent from its Pre-Collapse
popu-lation. Its highest population is 943 million at Isle, and its highest
tech level is 9 at Isle.

Isle (Daangiilu) Subsector (historical): The Isle (Daangiilu) Subsector had
an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 168 billion. Its highest
population was 95.1 billion at Isle, and its highest tech level was 16 at
Lukham.
