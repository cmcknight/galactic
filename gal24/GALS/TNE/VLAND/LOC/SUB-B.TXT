
VHODAN

Vhodan Subsector: The Vhodan Subsector has an estimated population in 1202
of approximately 1.66 billion, a decline of 97 percent from its
Pre-Collapse popu-lation. Its highest population is 584 million at Diiron,
and its highest tech level is 9 at Timat.

Vhodan Subsector (historical): The Vhodan Subsector had an estimated
population in 1117 of approximately 58.28 billion. Its highest population
was 41.8 billion at Guusimka, and its highest tech level was 15 at
Otsaellghue and Maran.
