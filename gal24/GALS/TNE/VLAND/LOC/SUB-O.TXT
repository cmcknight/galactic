
ISTUARY

Istuary Subsector: The Istuary Subsector has an estimated population in
1202 of approximately 5.17 billion, a decline of 84 percent from its
Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 4.94 billion at Segikin,
and its highest tech level is 11 at Istuary.

Istuary (Nulisud) Subsector (historical): The Istuary (Nulisud) Subsector
had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 31.41 billion. Its
highest population was 9.34 billion at Dusekhip, and its highest tech level
was 15 at Miku, Nulisud and Hykluitt.
