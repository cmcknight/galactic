Drayrk (Imperial UWP B150578-C)
       (Human    UWP C150655-8)

Drayrk economy collapsed 1125 to Tl9 and a second time 1127 to Tl8,
and was therefore not infected. The worlds suffered a starvation
of 81% and 66% mainly Vilani and Lancian settlers.

The former balkanized world is now ruled by a moderate technocracy
of land barons owning the few water reservoirs of the world. About
three quarter of the population are Jonkeereen settled there to counter
Suerrat expansion early in the 400s by SuSAG.

The world received a tradeport 1175 but decided not to upgrade their
economy to dynamical linked systems.
