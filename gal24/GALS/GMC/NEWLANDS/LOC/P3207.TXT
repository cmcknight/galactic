IMSC NOTICE OF HAZARDOUS NAVIGATION ZONE
Location: Porrilla, Newlands 3207
Nature of Hazard: Neutron Star
Date of notification: 012-2193T

The Porrilla system is centred on a neutron star and as such posses a
significant hazard to navigation. Vessels entering the system should procead
with extreme caution at all times. Ships should stay within the marked
navigation lanes at all times and comply with all instructions from the System
Control Authority. Failure to do so may result in severe damage or loss due to
the high levels of radiation present in the system. The local administration
provides regular updates on hazardous regions and system conditions. It is
strongly advised that any incoming vessel avails themselves of these services.
