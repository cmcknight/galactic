Spearhead Subsector
-------------------
This well settled subsector serves as a lateral and horizontal communications
route with x-boat links passing both ways. This region has extensive trade
activity and a great deal of starship traffic. All worlds in this area are
either full Imperium members or unihabited protectorates.

The Spearhead subsector contains 34 worlds with a total population of 295.5
billion. The highest population is A, at Redcross, Noscitur, Amelek, Passage,
and Umpopula; the highest tech level is C, at Hrnatis, Unconbuts, and Wilmot.
