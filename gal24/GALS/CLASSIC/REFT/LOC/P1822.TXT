NEUBAYERN
"New Bavaria"

Marinus van der Lubbe reached Neubayern in 4516.

In 4860, Marinus van der Lubbe and Voyageur, in company, return
to Neubayern.

Topas, Herzenlust, and Berlichingen were settled from Neubayern.

One of the three original worlds settled, Neubayern remained on
the fringes of development until jump drives became available. 
Thereafter, it has joined in the competition for its share of the
Cluster.  Neubayern is governed by a charismatic oligarchy, and
has one colony:  Schlesien Belt.

Neubayern established a base in previously uninhabited Schlesien
Belt to exploit its resources.
