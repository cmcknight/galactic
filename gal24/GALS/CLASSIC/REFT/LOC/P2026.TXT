COLCHIS
an ancient country in Asia S of the Caucasus and bordering on the
Black Sea:  the land of the Golden Fleece and of 
Medea in Greek legend (Webster’s)

In AD 4788, New Home launched its first interstellar spaceship
(Outward Bound) with an expedition to colonize Colchis.  Outward
Bound planted the first colony on Colchis in 4814.

New Colchis was settled from Colchis.
