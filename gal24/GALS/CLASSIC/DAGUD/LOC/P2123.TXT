Although the planet primarily deals in groat ranching, the planet is best 
known for its Redleaf plants. Yana, a highly addictive drug can be 
extracted from the leaves. It is illegal to possess, own or trade in Yana,

complex laws surrounding the whole subject have driven at least one well
