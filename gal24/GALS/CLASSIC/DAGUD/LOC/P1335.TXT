
Kiirishi     1335 C6A4223-B    Lo Ni Fl       104 Im K7 V

Gas giant satellite, the atmosphere is composed of an irritant gas. A 
small colony was established deep within the planetary crust which 
exports rare chemicals found on the surface. The population all wear 
full face masks in public. It is believed this custom evolved from 
forced wearing of repirators over the years. The natives believe an 
uncovered face to be obscene.
