
Aiaiyal   1821  A572845-E		 710  Im K2 II

Was settled in -877 by the Aohalai and Oakhtai'yal clans as a
second-generation colony of the Aslan community on Medurma. The world is now
an important exporter of high-tech electronics and computer systems and
houses drydock and ship construction facilities for Aohalai sector-wide
trading firm Aospace.
Aiaiyal is a true example of peaceful, constructive cooperation between
Humans and Aslan, with each group maintaining its own, distinctive culture.
