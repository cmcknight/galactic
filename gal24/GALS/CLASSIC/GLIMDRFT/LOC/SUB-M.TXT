Verge Subsector
---------------
The Verge subsector is the furthest trailing extension of Imperium space. It
is an off-shoot of the area known as the Old Expanses. Herot is a member of
the Krax Confederation while Kildonovan and Istok are independent. The scene
of much altercation in the past, over the last several decades, the frontiers
have stabilised.

The Verge subsector contains 26 worlds with a total population of 125 billion.
The highest population is A, at Frisney, Franklin, and Kildonovan; the
highest Tech level is C, at Mou, Zucko, Icewind, and Kildonovan.
