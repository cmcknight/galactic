Poonch Subsector
----------------
The Poonch subsector is mostly occupied by the Krax Confederation with 6
members of the Zarian Realm on the rimward edge. The 8 systems inbetween
have an independent stance but look more towards the former than the latter.

The Poonch subsector contains 30 worlds with a total population of 1 billion.
The highest population is 8, at Afin and Poonch; the highest Tech level is E
at Afin.
