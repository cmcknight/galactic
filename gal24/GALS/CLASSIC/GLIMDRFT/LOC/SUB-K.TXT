Sosie Subsector
---------------
The Sosie subsector has a scattering of independent worlds with an intrusion
of Krax Confederation systems in the spinward core corner. This area has been
the scene of several recent clashes over the resources of undeveloped planets.

The Sosie subsector contains 25 worlds with a total population of 30 billion.
The highest population is A, at Sosie; the highest Tech level is D, at Abin.
