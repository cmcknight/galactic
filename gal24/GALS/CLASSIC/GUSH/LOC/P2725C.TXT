
    Trici University

    Trici  University on  Moytra is  best known  for the  excellence of its
    social  science  courses.  Particularly  of  note  are  its journalism,
    history,  and  psychology  courses.  Professor  Dittmeyer  Valensina of
    Trici's  history  faculty  was  the  recipient  of  the 1114 Alkhalikoi
    Imperial  Histroy prize  for his  thesis on  the Sylean Federation. The
    University, located in the heart of downtown Trici ( Moytra's capital )
    was  the first  establishment in  Gushemege to  offer an  undergraduate
    course in psychohistory.
