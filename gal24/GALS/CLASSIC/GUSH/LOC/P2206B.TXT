
    IRPA

    IRPA is  the powerful trade alliance  formed between Irkhi (  2207 In )
    and  Painntu  (  2206  Ag  ).  The  alliance  dominates  industrial and
    agricultural  trade  in  the  Painntu  cluster.  A  new class A orbital
    starport is being  built at Painntu in order  to increase exports still
    further.
