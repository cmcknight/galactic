
    Arparp Contruction LIC

    Arparp  Construction is  a major  civil construction  company operating
    throughout the Heartland and parts of Rurevayn. The company was founded
    in 943 by a group of engineering graduates from UTI. Their headquarters
    are on Droun and the company made its name for building the Marou tower
    on Zanish.  The company is currently  engaged on a Starport  upgrade at
    Ushuuri and  the building of  a hoop arcology  for 1 million  people on
    Oaxaca.
