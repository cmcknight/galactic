
    Kadar Business School

    The KBS  is the foremost  centre for business  and commercial study  in
    Gushemege.  Its Hkahri  Study Centre  is a  highly regarded  commercial
    consultancy  and several  of its   members have  held CEO  positions in
    megacorporation  Sector   offices.  Unlike  the   more  finance  minded
    establishments such as Gigimu and  Lumuiinshi, at Kadar the emphasis is
    on the  whole gamut of business  skills including management, strategic
    planning, informatics, and leadership.
