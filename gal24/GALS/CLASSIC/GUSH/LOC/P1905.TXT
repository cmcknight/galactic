
    Trama ( 1905 B7B0510-E Ni De )                               circa 1116

    The corporate world  of Trama rebelled against Imperial  rule in 980 in
    an  attempt  to  free  itself  from  Imperial  restrictions  on psionic
    research. The world was brought  back ino line by blockade. Makhidkarun
    now oversees  the operations of  Trama Oy. The  company is active  in a
    wide range of consumer and  domestic electronics and the entertainments
    market.
