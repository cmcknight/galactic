
    Unlardigard Technology Institute

    UTI is the foremost technical  institute in Gushemege. Its specialities
    are  engineering, mining  and  archaeotechnology  ( and  the associated
    reverse  engineering  ).  The  UTI's  Merara  Foundation  is  a leading
    investigator of Ancient sites; but UTI's  real value to the sector lies
    in  its  practical  academic  support  to  the  industrial  worlds  and
    corporations of the Heartland.
