
    Greunemir

    Greunemir are  an Imperium wide environmental  movement with their base
    on  Jasper.  Greunemir  are  a  pro-active  organisation  whose members
    regularly risk  life and limb  to block anti-environmental  operations.
    Key activities  in the last  decade include the  protest against biowar
    testing  on  Salliatt,  and   against  the  neurotech  fishing  methods
    introduced on Oaxaca/Truax.
