   I have yet to do this for the post second survey (ie. Rebellion) data
   since it is by definition incomplete and changing.
   
   Any allegiance code eclosed in parentheses and with question mark
   indicates that I have some question as to the validity. This is mostly
   because I can not recall all of the sources (and their official
   status). Any sector names in parentheses indicate that the government
   apparently overlaps other sector(s) but they are not published. In
   cases where an allegiance overlaps only a single sector border, I have
   tried to show both sector names. For allegiances which enter more than
   two sectors, I have used the sector name which has the greatest extent
   of the state. Due to my posting of credits/sources after the sector, I
   have restricted all multi-sector listings to the single greatest
   extent of the state.

   listing for the New Era version yet to be completed. I have included
   all of those allegiance codes in this list as well.
